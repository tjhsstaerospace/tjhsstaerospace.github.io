What we do

TJ Rocketry Club is a student run rocketry club!

Building Rockets

We work with our mentors to build rockets from scratch using laser cutters, 3D printers and other tools.

Competition

At TJ Rocketry Club, we compete in many exciting competitions such as Team America Rocketry Challenge and Battle of the Rockets. We also mentor L1-Certification and special projects such as dual deploy.

Friendships

At TJ Rocketry Club, we make more than rockets. Being a part of our community connects you with other TJ aerospace nerds and with alumni with useful experience.
